Animal Classifier | DenseNet121

This is an animal classifier that is capable of classifying 55 species of animals, listed down below Select an image and upload it to the model, which will then try to classify the image

This classifier is a DenseNet121 model made and trained in PyTorch, on a dataset of over 2000 animal images from Google. This model was the converted to ONNX and then to Tensorflow and Tensorflow.js.

Privacy Statement

All image processing is done offline, your images never get sent over the internet.
